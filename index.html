    • About Us

    Peter Wilkin has been a professional ceramic artist since 1983.

    Peter started making pots in high school, and has never stopped.


    Peter produces:

  • Functional Pots
  • Decorative Chargers
  • Customizeable platters and slab installations

    • Eva got her start in clay in high school and is a Kansas City Art Institute ceramics graduate.


    She works in porcelain making:

  • Functional Pots
  • Bowls

    • She likes lightweight elegant forms, and has fun decorating them with her face designs.


    The baby bowl is the only pot that Peter and Eva make together. Peter throws the porcelain and Eva decorates it.


    Peter and Eva both studied under Ken Ferguson, Victor Babu, and George Timock at the Kansas City Art Institute.

    Peter has also taught at KCAI and JCCC


    Our philosophy is to make the best, most useful pots we can. We both love to cook and use pots for everything. So should you!
